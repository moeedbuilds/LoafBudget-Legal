# Privacy Policy — Loaf Budget

**Effective date:** June 25, 2026
**Provider:** Moeed Faisal ("we", "us", "our")
**Contact:** support@loafbudget.app

Loaf Budget ("the app") helps you understand your daily cost of living. We built it to
collect as little as possible. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices
you have.

## 1. Information we collect

**Account information.** When you create an account we collect your **email address**. If
you sign in with **Apple** or **Google**, we receive your email and, optionally, your name
from that provider. We never receive your Apple or Google password.

**Budgeting data you enter.** The app stores the budgeting data you create: expenses
(amounts, descriptions, dates, and the loaf each is filed under), income, the accounts you
add, "loaves" (the budget envelopes the app is built around), and cash-balance entries.
This is the core content of the app and is tied to your account. We do **not** connect to
your bank or import your transactions — you enter your data yourself.

**Technical information.** The minimum needed to operate the service — for example, the
authentication tokens that keep you signed in, and basic error and connection state. Your
signed-in session is **encrypted at rest** on your device, with the encryption key held in
the device's secure keychain.

**Crash and error diagnostics.** The app integrates **Sentry**, an error-reporting tool,
to capture technical information about crashes and errors (such as the error type, device
and OS version, and non-personal app-state codes) so we can find and fix problems. Sentry
is configured to **never** collect your budgeting data, name, email address, or any
personal identifiers, and we do not use it for advertising or to track you.

**We do _not_ collect:** advertising identifiers, location, contacts or your address book,
or behavioral/usage analytics. The app contains **no advertising SDKs and no cross-app
tracking**, and we do not build advertising profiles of you.

## 2. How we use your information

- To provide the app: authenticate you, and store and sync your budget across your devices.
- To operate, secure, and maintain the service (keep you signed in, prevent unauthorized
  access, diagnose crashes, and fix bugs).
- To respond to support requests you send us.

We do **not** use your information for advertising, profiling, or tracking across apps or
websites, and we do **not** sell or rent your information.

## 3. How your information is stored and protected

Your account data is stored with our cloud database provider, **Supabase**, on servers in
**the United States**. Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS), and access
is restricted per-account using row-level security, so your data is reachable only by your
authenticated account. Before you sign in — and whenever you are signed out — the app keeps
your data only in local storage on your own device.

## 4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell or rent your information. We share it only with the service providers that
operate the app on our behalf, and only as needed:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and sync.
- **Apple** and **Google** — only if you choose to sign in with them (identity).
- **Sentry** — crash and error diagnostics (technical data only, as described above).

We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or
security of our users or the service.

## 5. Data retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. You can **delete your account at any
time** from **Settings → Delete account**. Deleting your account permanently removes your
account and all associated data — expenses, income, loaves, accounts, and cash history.
This cannot be undone.

## 6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete
your personal data. You can **export your data as a CSV file** at any time from **Settings
→ Export data**, and you can **delete everything yourself** via in-app account deletion.
For any other request, contact us at **support@loafbudget.app**.

## 7. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your
country), and we do not knowingly collect their information.

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Effective date" above and,
for material changes, provide notice within the app.

## 9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: **support@loafbudget.app**.
